About the engineer

The Data engineer is a profile dedicated to the processing of massive data. his role, among other things, is to enhance all of the company's data (predictive models, etc.). He analyzes masses of heterogeneous data, possibly unstructured, to extract useful knowledge for the optimization of the offers, services and processes of the company. His skills in the processing and exploitation of structured or semi-structured data allow him to intervene on subjects relating to Business Intelligence and decision-making computer systems.

The Data engineer also has the role of creating and optimizing the infrastructures of storage, manipulation and restitution of raw data. He works in concert with the teams of development, Business Intelligence and system administration, designing functional solutions, anticipating future needs and respecting the constraints imposed by existing architectures.
